During my life at MRSM, I found a lot of friends and all of them are really kind and taking a good care of me at MRSM. I really love all of them. When you are studying in boarding school, you do not have your family members to take care of you, so you totally depend on your friends and teachers. During my senior year, I have a very hectic schedule as all mrsm students are preparing for SPMRSM which is known as the hardest exam for primary school students besides, we also need to prepare for SPM that will happen around the corner. The stress is there, but with the help of family, teachers, and my lovely friends I do manage to excel the exam. All praise to Allah to make me enjoy all this moment with all my friends. Now, all of this become a good memory that I will keep as my precious treasure of MRSM life. Thank you, my friends.
